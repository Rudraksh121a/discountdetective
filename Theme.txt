In general, this style can be called a "Dark Premium Theme" or "Luxurious Dark Theme". It has these key characteristics:

Dark Backgrounds: Deep blues or dark navies for a sophisticated base.

Accent Colors: Rich, bold colors like red or gold for a premium touch.

Text Colors: Soft whites or greys for contrast and readability.

Minimalistic Design: Clean, modern fonts and simple layouts.

Elegant Shadows: Subtle but noticeable to create depth.
